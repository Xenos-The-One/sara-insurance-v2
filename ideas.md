# Sara Life Insurance — Design Brainstorm

## Design Approaches

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Corporate Modernism meets Warm Trust
**Core Principles:**
1. Deep navy as the authoritative foundation — communicates stability and professionalism
2. Gold accents used sparingly as "trust signals" — CTAs, highlights, stars
3. Generous whitespace with structured asymmetric layouts — avoids generic grid monotony
4. Photography-first thinking — circular headshots, lifestyle imagery, human warmth

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: #1a365d (deep navy) — authority, protection, trust
- Accent: #2b6cb0 (steel blue) — approachability, clarity
- CTA Gold: #d69e2e — premium, urgency without aggression
- Warm white #f7f8fc backgrounds — clean without clinical coldness
- Emotional intent: "I'm a professional you can trust with your family's future"

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: Left-aligned bold headline, right-side lifestyle illustration/photo
- Sections alternate between full-width navy backgrounds and white card grids
- Sticky nav with transparent-to-solid scroll transition
- Feature cards with left-border gold accent lines

**Signature Elements:**
1. Gold horizontal rule dividers between major sections
2. Circular photo frames with navy ring borders
3. Step-number indicators in large gold numerals

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll with section entrance animations (fade-up)
- CTA buttons with subtle gold shimmer on hover
- Accordion FAQ with smooth expand/collapse

**Animation:**
- Fade-up on scroll for cards and sections (staggered)
- Nav background transitions from transparent to white on scroll
- Progress bar animation on quote form steps

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) for hero headlines — gravitas
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) for all body text — readability
- Hierarchy: 56px hero / 36px section / 24px card / 16px body
</idea>

## Selected Design: **Corporate Modernism meets Warm Trust** (Response 1)

This approach best matches the brief: premium, credible, trustworthy, yet approachable. The Playfair Display + Inter pairing creates authority without coldness. Gold accents on CTAs drive conversions without feeling aggressive. The asymmetric hero layout avoids the generic centered insurance website look.
